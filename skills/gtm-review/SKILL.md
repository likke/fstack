# GTM Review

## What This Does
Before any campaign, outreach sequence, or new channel goes live — run it through this review. Prevents spending Charlene's (or your) time on campaigns that won't close.

## The Three Questions

### 1. Does it reach the right person with the right pain?
Define your best client profile:
- Industry/sector
- Role (who has the pain AND the budget)
- Specific pain (what specific failure are they experiencing?)
- Budget signal (can they afford your standard package?)

Does this campaign reach that exact person? If not → wrong target.

### 2. Are we selling outcome or feature?
The rule: **sell the outcome, never the feature.**

Wrong: "AI-powered content creation platform"
Wrong: "Unlimited content packages"
Right: "Your campaigns ship on time, every time, with zero revision loops"

Does the message lead with outcome? If not → rewrite it.

### 3. Revenue Gate
- **A: Likely to close in 30 days** — right person, budget signal, decision-maker reached
- **B: Likely to close in 60-90 days** — right ICP, early signal
- **C: Brand building** — no direct close, runs on automation
- **D: Wrong ICP or wrong message** → kill immediately

Only A and B get human time. C runs on automation.

## Review Output Format
```
CAMPAIGN/CHANNEL: [describe]

CLIENT TEST: Pass / Fail — [right person + right pain?]
OUTCOME TEST: Pass / Fail — [selling outcome not feature?]
REVENUE GATE: A / B / C / D

RECOMMENDATION: Launch / Rewrite / Defer / Kill
ONE-LINE REASON: [why]

IF LAUNCH:
- Target: [exact persona + sector]
- Hook: [first line that stops the scroll]
- CTA: [one action only — never two]
- Owner: [who runs this — human or automated?]
- Success signal: [how do we know in 2 weeks if this is working?]
```

## GTM Rules
- Never mention the product in the first 3 touchpoints. Lead with the pain.
- Your best client's results are your only case study. Use them every time.
- One CTA only. If you have two CTAs, you have zero CTAs.
- Disqualify anyone who asks for a custom workflow on the first call. They are not your client.
- No free consulting. The discovery call IS the audit.
- Thirty-day paid pilot = the close. Lowers resistance, proves value.
- No discounts. If they can't afford your standard rate, they can't afford your team's time.
